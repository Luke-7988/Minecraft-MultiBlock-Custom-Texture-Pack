2. Ultimate Datapack

Welcome to the world's first MULTI-BLOCK CUSTOM texture pack! This pack runs in VANILLA Minecraft, without need of any other installations. It allows the texture to appear once the system detects a certain configuration. For example, if the configuration is a 2*2*2 cube of iron blocks, then when these iron blocks are placed, your custom texture comes out! It is highly recommended to AI generate textures (suggested dimensions would be 16-64 pixels per block). Your initial picture might be horizontal, but just compress it into a square. Textures have to be in PNG format.

Run this command in-game to get your stick!
Toggles between texture, original blocks, and no texture (nothing)

Windows Warning!

If you are on Windows and the unzipped folder appears empty, you may need to "unblock" the file. Right-click the downloaded .zip file, go to Properties, and in the General tab, check the "Unblock" box at the bottom and click "Apply" and "OK". Then, try unzipping again.
